Grade 9 Subject Guidance

Choosing the right subjects in Grade 9 is important for your future career.

  • Science Path: Choose Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Maths
  • Commerce Path: Choose Accounting, Business Studies, Maths/Math Lit
  • Humanities Path: Choose History, Geography, Languages
